How to prepare tasty nigeria jollof

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 plum tomatoes
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 1 red onion
  • 4 plum tomatoes
  • 1 thumb size fresh ginger
  • 2 garlic gloves
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1/4 cup sunflower oil
  • 1 cup long grain rice (washed)
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup of vegetable stock

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. chop the tomatoes,onions and bell pepper imto chucks. place into a blendeer/food processor together with the ginger,garlic, and scotch bonnet pepper, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. place a pot over medium heat, pour into the sunflower oil, and add the blended mixture.stir and allow to sizzle, then place a lid over it, reduce heat to low and simmer for 20mins.
  3. after simmering for 20 mins, remove the lid, then add the spices and seasonings(paprika,thyme, nutmeg, bayleaf and salt). stir altogether.
  4. also pour in the rice and vegetable or meat stock and stir altogether
  5. place a piece of alumininum foil into the pot,covering the entire surface of the rice (this will keep the steam in) then place a lid over the pot, cook for 20 mins on low heat (stirring occasionally to cook the rice evenly and also to prevent it from burning at the bottom).
  6. remove lid, bin the alumininum foil and give the rice a gentle stir, then return the lid and cook for another 5 mins
  7. now the rice should be ready to serve . serve hot with your favorite slides and enjoy.
